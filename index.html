We are a community of spiritual friends supporting each other in awareness

About our practice

The purpose of this group will be to support one another in building and maintaining awareness through meditation and discussion. The group will be rooted in Buddhist teachings and guided by Buddhist ethics, prioritizing a spirit of kindness and friendship above any particular belief or dogma. Recognizing that our members hail from a variety of meditation backgrounds, we look forward to sharing our thoughts and experiences and listening to one another with awareness and sensitivity--while maintaining strict confidentiality and respect for one another's privacy when we leave the space.

As a group of experienced meditators, we will not have a guided meditation, though some members may like to share favorite teachings on the subject, and our meditative experiences will come up in group discussions. During the periods of silent medidation, each member will medidate according to their own personal practice. Though we will not have a regular teacher, we may optionally choose to bring in guest teachers or special guests as opportunity permits.

Facilitation

We will strive to maintain two active facilitators to build resiliency and sound judgment into the leadership of the group. Facilitation will be guided by Spirit Rock's guidelines on Kalyana Mitta (spiritual friend) groups. Though facilitators will maintain the structure of the sessions, our hope is that every member of the group feels free to offer suggestions or to take on various operational roles in the group.

Donations

We will put out a dana box with a suggested donation of $5, all of which will go to our hosts to help with costs. Members may donate more than the suggested amount or nothing at all - no questions asked.

What to expect

Initially, regular sessions will be structured according to this schedule:

  • (20 minutes) Silent Mediation
  • (15 mins) Check-in/Introductions
  • (40 mins) Offering of Teachings
  • (15 mins) Movement/Walking Meditation
  • (15-30 mins) Dharma Sharing, Questions, Group Business, Misc.

The group may decide to introduce new elements or to change the schedule of the sessions as we settle into regular group practice.

Time and place

We are an in-person group. We currently meet on Sundays from 10am to 12pm at Wayfinder Wellness in Friday Harbor, WA. We urge you to join our facebook group to get updates on session dates and locations.
